Pre‑biosigns (pre‑biological signals) are measurable changes in your body that appear before you feel sick — a slight drop in white blood cells, a tiny rise in liver enzymes, a change in nerve protein levels. They're not symptoms; they're data.

This tool tracks these signals across six organ systems, helps you spot dangerous patterns, and generates doctor‑ready scripts — so you can act before a crisis.

Toxicology & Emergency Protocols

⚡ Anaphylaxis: Epinephrine 0.3 mg IM → Dexamethasone 40 mg IV
🫁 Aspiration: Thickened liquids, upright positioning
🔬 Heavy metals: NAC, selenium, magnesium — never self-chelate
💊 QT drugs: Avoid macrolides + fluoroquinolones concurrently
🩸 Cd/As exposure: Sources — tobacco, rice, contaminated water
🧠 B-vitamin neuropathy: Check serum B1, B6, B12 levels urgently
🆘 Poison Control: 1-800-222-1222 🚑 Emergency: 911
